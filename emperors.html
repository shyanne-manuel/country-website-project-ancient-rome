List of Ancient Roman Emperors
Emperor Reign Duration Succession Life Details
Tiberius 14-37 C.E. Stepson, former son-in-law and adopted son of Augustus 42 B.C.E. - 37 C.E.
Died probably of natural causes, alledgedly murdered at the instigation of Caligula
Caligula 37-41 C.E. Grandnephew and adopted heir of Tiberius, great-grandson of Augustus 12-41 C.E. Murdered in the conspiracy incvolving the Praetorian Guard and senators
Claudius 41-54 C.E. Uncle of Caligula, grandnephew of Augustus 10 B.C.E. - 54 C.E. Probably poisoned by his wife Agrippina, in favor of her son Nero
Nero 54-68 C.E. Grandnephew, stepson, son-in-law and adopted son of Claudius, great-grandson of Augustus 37-68 C.E. Commited suicide after being deserted by the Praetorian Guard and sentenced to death by the Senate.
